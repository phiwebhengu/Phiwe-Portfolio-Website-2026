About the Game Jam

ARCADEMIA: Myth, Folklore & Legends was a 48-hour international game jam held from 27 February to 1 March 2026, hosted across five universities: North-West University, University of Lincoln, Wits, UP, and the University of Hull. The challenge was to design a playable game inspired by ancient tales and modern myths — a story reborn through interactivity.

Working as the Technical Artist and Game Developer, I collaborated with my team to design, build, and ship a complete game in just 48 hours from the Wits Digital Arts Building. The best games would be installed on Arcademia arcade cabinets at partner universities worldwide — and ours was one of them. We won First Place.
